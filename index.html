CodeChef CodeChef is a competitive programming website. It is a non-profit educational initiative of Unacademy,[1][2][3] aimed at providing a platform for students, young software professionals to practice, hone their programming skills through online contests[4] Apart from this, the 'CodeChef For Schools' program aims to reach out to young students and inculcate a culture of programming in Indian schools.[5]
